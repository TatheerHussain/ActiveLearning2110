Login and Register Account

  • Enter your email address you signed up with and password.
  • Click Login
  • If you don't have an account, press Register
  • Enter your first and last name and Georgia Tech email address and then choose a password.
Note that as a student, you don't need a Professor Key so you may ignore that field and press Register when required fields are complete.
